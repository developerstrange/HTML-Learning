When did tacos first appear in Bangladesh?

Tacos likely made their first appearance in Bangladesh in the late 2000s to early 2010s, mainly through international fast food chains, expat communities, and the growing popularity of global cuisine in Dhaka and other major cities. There is no official record of the exact first taco served.(Source: Tacos of Dhaka)
